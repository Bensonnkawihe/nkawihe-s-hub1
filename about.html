Who We Are

Nkawihe’s Hub is a registered and trusted business based in Mponela, Malawi. We specialize in two major sectors: quality phone accessories and fresh farm produce. Our commitment is to offer reliable products, fair prices, and excellent customer service.

What We Offer

  • Phone chargers, cables, earphones, power banks
  • Phone cases, screen protectors, repair tools
  • Maize, beans, groundnuts, soya beans

Our Vision

To become one of the leading suppliers of electronics and agricultural products in Malawi.
